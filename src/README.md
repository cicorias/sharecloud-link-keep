# LinkVault - iOS Share Link Capture

## How to Use

### Setting Up on iOS

1. **Add to Home Screen**:
   - Open the app in Safari
   - Tap the share icon at the bottom of the screen
   - Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
   - Tap "Add" in the top right corner

2. **Set Up for Link Sharing**:
   - When you find a link you want to save:
     - Tap the share icon in Safari (or any app)
     - Find LinkVault in the share options (you may need to tap "More" and enable it)
     - Select LinkVault and the link will be saved automatically

### Features

- **Organize with Categories**: Create custom categories to organize your links
- **Quick Search**: Find saved links instantly with the search bar
- **Tag System**: Add multiple tags to each link for better organization
- **Share Links**: Easily share your saved links with others

### Technical Details

LinkVault uses your device's local storage to save links, so they'll be available even when you're offline. No data is sent to external servers.

## Privacy

This app stores all data locally on your device. No information is collected or transmitted externally.
