Getting Started

Save Links

Click the LinkVault icon in your browser toolbar or use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Shift+S (⌘+Shift+S on Mac) to save the current page.

Organize with Categories

Add categories to your links for better organization and quick filtering when you need to find something.

Sync Across Devices

Your links automatically sync with the LinkVault mobile app, so you can access your collection from anywhere.
